# AI Model Card: MarketPulse Sentiment Engine

## Model Details
- **Architecture**: 10-stage NLP processing pipeline combining lexicon-based scoring, named entity recognition (NER), and rule-based heuristic aggregation.
- **Version**: `rule_based_v1`
- **Release Date**: 2026

## Intended Use
- Analyzing short-form financial news and social media sentiment.
- Extracting relevant entities (Stocks, Cryptos).
- Generating transparent Bullish/Bearish signal components.

## Limitations & Risks
- **Not Predictive**: High sentiment does NOT guarantee positive price movement.
- **Sarcasm Detection**: The current heuristic pipeline struggles with sarcasm and nuanced financial rhetoric.
- **Context Loss**: News sentiment is isolated and does not account for macroeconomic factors unless explicitly mentioned in the text.

## Weights & Transparency
Signals are generated by combining the following scores:
- Technical Score (40%)
- Fundamental Score (30%)
- News Sentiment Score (20%)
- Market Context (10%)

*Note: These weights are configurable via the admin dashboard and can be adjusted per asset class.*
